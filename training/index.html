How To Get Access

To gain swipe access into the Brandeis MakerLab, we ask that you attend one of our weekly training workshops in 3D Printing, 3D Scanning, or Embeded Systems and Soldering.

To attend a trainings, email makerlab@brandeis.edu at least 24 hours before the training begins to reserve a spot. Please show up on time as we need the full 2 hours to cover all the topics! If you can't make any of the specified training times, email us and we'll schedule something that works for you.

Topic Time
Intro to 3D printing Friday from 10am-12pm
Intro to 3D scanning Thursday from 3pm-5pm
Soldering training Monday from 2pm-4pm
